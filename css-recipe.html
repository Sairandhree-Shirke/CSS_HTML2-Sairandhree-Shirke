Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Tomato
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions For Sandwich Making:

  1. Cook bacon in a skillet until crispy brown , then set aside.
  2. Wash the tomato and make a thin slice of it.
  3. Now take bread and butter one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, on a sandwitch maker over medium heat.
  5. Layer cheddar cheese, bacon, and tomato slices on the bread.
  6. Top with the other slice of bread, butter side up.
  7. Cook until the bottom bread is golden brown, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from sandwichmaker and serve hot.
